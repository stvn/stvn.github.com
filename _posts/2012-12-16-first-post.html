Fun with jekyll and and github pages!

Jekyll is a static content authoring tool, that when used with github pages, makes it dead simple (and sexy) to create a blog.

Just getting set up and trying to figure out what this bad boy can do. So far it's been really easy to get going. I initialially started out using jekyll bootstrap, however, I quickly tired trying to make it look less twitter-bootstrap-like. Instead, I ripped everthing out and started from scratch. Ulitmately, I'm pretty happy with the result. I wanted something simple and functional, and in the end, I think that's what I ended up with.

It's been over 5 years since last maintaining a blog. I often find myself thinking about starting to do it again and then life distracts me. So, today I got up and decided not to be distracted. And now, I write...
